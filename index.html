How to use AMP4 YouTube to MP4 Converter

  1. You visit YouTube.com and find any kind of video, which you would like to convert to MP4. You then copy the video URL from the upper URL bar in your browser. If you are on Android phone or an iPhone, you click share icon bellow the video, and then click on copy link button.
  2. You then go to my website and paste the copied URL to the input field.

  5. When the conversion is finished, a download button will appear. You click on this button, and your video will start to download. You can then click on Convert another button, to convert / download another YouTube video.
